1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email, password, profile picture, bio, location
  • User Content: Posts, messages, products, reviews, comments
  • Communication: Messages, support requests, feedback
  • Biometric Data: Facial descriptors (if facial recognition enabled)
  • Payment Information: If applicable, processed securely by third parties

Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, OS, browser, unique identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used, clicks
  • Location Data: IP address, approximate location (with consent)
  • Log Data: Access times, error information, referral URLs
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session data, preferences, analytics

Derived Data

  • User preferences and behavior patterns
  • Engagement metrics and recommendations
  • Aggregated insights and trends
  • De-identified usage statistics

2. How We Use Information

Life uses collected information to:

  • Operate the Service: Create accounts, process transactions, provide support
  • Improve Experience: Personalize content, recommendations, features
  • Maintain Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, enforce policies
  • Develop Features: Research, analytics, product improvement
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with laws, respond to requests, enforce agreements
  • Marketing: Send promotions, updates (with consent)
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns, improve service quality

3. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

Life may use User Content to train and improve AI and machine learning systems:

  • Feature Improvement: Enhance recommendations, search, personalization
  • AI Training: Develop new AI capabilities and models
  • Data Retention: Aggregated or de-identified data retained indefinitely
  • Private Content: Private or restricted content only used if disclosed
AI Transparency: Life is building features powered by AI and machine learning. Your content may be used to improve these systems. You can opt-out through your privacy settings if applicable.

4. Commercial Use

Life may use your data and content for:

  • Improving algorithms and recommendations
  • Developing new products and services
  • Promoting the platform and features
  • Internal research and analytics

Important: Life does NOT sell identifiable content as standalone media without separate consent. Aggregated, anonymized, or de-identified data may be used and shared freely.

5. Biometric Data (Facial Recognition)

What We Collect

If you enable facial recognition, Life collects:

  • Facial geometry and characteristics (128-dimensional descriptor)
  • No actual images are stored - only mathematical descriptors
  • Data is encrypted and securely stored

How We Use It

  • Authentication and login verification
  • Personalization and feature enhancement
  • Improving system accuracy and performance

Protection and Rights

  • Consent Required: Explicit opt-in through visual confirmation
  • Not Sold: Biometric identifiers are never sold
  • Delete Anytime: You can permanently delete biometric data
  • No Image Storage: Only mathematical descriptors retained
  • Secure Processing: All biometric data encrypted in transit and at rest
Biometric Privacy Rights
You have the right to access, delete, or disable facial recognition at any time. Your biometric data is your property and will not be used without your explicit consent.

6. Data Sharing

Life may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, support (under confidentiality)
  • Business Partners: For specific features or integrations (as disclosed)
  • Legal Compliance: Law enforcement, court orders (as required)
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, bankruptcy
  • Aggregated Data: De-identified or anonymized data shared freely

No Sale of Personal Data: Life does not sell personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Data Retention

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Correct: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Port: Request your data in portable format
  • Opt-Out: Withdraw consent for biometric or marketing uses
  • Restrict: Limit use of your data for specific purposes

To Exercise Your Rights: Contact privacy@life-app.local with your request.

9. Security

Life implements security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure biometric data storage with hashing
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Incident response procedures

Disclaimer: While we implement safeguards, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection.

10. Children

Life is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where Life operates. By using Life, you consent to such transfers.

12. Policy Updates

Life may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting. Continued use constitutes acceptance of updated policies.
